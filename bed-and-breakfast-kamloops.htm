Welcome To Lakeside Country Inn

At Bed and Breakfast Kamloops just let us know what date you would like to stay with us, and the type of room that you prefer. We are happy to recommend a room if you are not sure which room would be most suitable. We will check availability and e-mail you back as soon as possible. 

Choose from a lakeview room or a larger kitchen suite. Bed and breakfast rates and room only rates are available as listed.  A continental breakfast, including a wonderful selection of fruit salad, yogurt, cereals, muffins, and our English cream scones is served in the glass enclosed Garden Cafe during the summer.  It is a relaxing way to start the day.
