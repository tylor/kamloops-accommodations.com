Your hosts are Donn and Margaret Sherman. Lakeside Country Inn is a family business with a heritage of thirty years. We hope that you will enjoy your stay with us and will return often. "Margaret spricht Deutsch."

The weather in Savona is truly wonderful. Visitors to the area enjoy our hot, sunny summers. The average daily temperature in June is 25 degrees C, July is 29 degrees C, August is 28 degrees C and in September it is 22 degrees C. If it is raining in Savona, it is probably raining throughout the province as we receive an average of only 25 cm of precipitation annually.
