Enjoy Charming Country Bed and Breakfast Hospitality

Lakeside Country Inn's bed-breakfast accommodation is charming and comfortable. All nine, individually decorated rooms and suites have a private entrance, private ensuite bathroom, colour cable TV, telephone with data port, clock radio, coffee maker, complimentary coffee and tea, and all luxurious amenities you would expect from a quality bed and breakfast. While all rooms are non-smoking, smoking is allowed outdoors on your balcony or patio.

A continental breakfast, including a wonderful selection of fruit salad, yogurt, cereals, muffins, and our English cream scones is served in the glass enclosed Garden Cafe during the summer.
