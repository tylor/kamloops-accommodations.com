Looking for kamloops accomodations?

Welcome To Lakeside Country Inn

When it comes to Kamloops accomodations, a great choice is to stay at the Lakeside Inn Bed and Breakfast, situated by the water at one of British Columbia's favourite holiday destinations. You're sure to love the hospitality of the charming Inn on Kamloops Lake, close to the City of Kamloops BC

- Enjoy kamloops sunset lake views from your private patio.
- Enjoy a relaxing overnight stay.
- Spend a romantic weekend get away.
- Take a week out to exploring British Columbia's Interior.

Choose from lakeview, queen bed and breakfast rooms, twin bed and breakfast rooms or larger kitchen suites. Bed and breakfast and room only rates are available, and while you don't have to stay for breakfast, it's a culinary treat you'll not want to miss.

A continental breakfast, including a wonderful selection of fruit salad, yogurt, cereals, muffins, and our English cream scones is served in the glass enclosed Garden Cafe during the summer. It is a relaxing way to start the day.

Select the type of room that you prefer and reserve the dates you would like to stay from our accomodations reservation form We are happy to recommend a room if you are not sure which room would be most suitable. We will check availability and e-mail you back as soon as possible
