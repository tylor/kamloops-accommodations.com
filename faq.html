Frequently Asked Questions

We have been operating Lakeside Country Inn for almost twenty four years and have been asked the same questions many times.  So for those of you that don’t have the time to email us your questions or think that you would be bothering us, we hope that these Frequently Asked Questions will be of some help.  And of course, please don’t hesitate to email or call if you have questions that are not answered here.  It would be our pleasure to answer your questions.

Will I be able to have dinner in Savona?

The Roadhouse Café is an authentic small town restaurant that serves breakfast all day, lunch and dinner.  The food is good, the prices are reasonable and the service is friendly.  

If you are looking for more ambiance, the Clubhouse at the Tobiano Golf Course is open during the golfing season.  A great view, great food and great service make this one of our favourite restaurants.  The Clubhouse is about 10 minutes away by car.  Check out their menu by visiting their web site at www.tobianogolf.com/clubhouse/ 

Please check with us regarding restaurant hours.
