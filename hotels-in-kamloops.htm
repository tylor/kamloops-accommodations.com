Welcome To Lakeside Country Inn

The weather is truly wonderful. Visitors to Hotels in Kamloops enjoy the area enjoy our hot, sunny summers. The average daily temperature in June is 25 degrees C, July is 29 degrees C, August is 28 degrees C and in September, it is 22 degrees C. If it is raining here, it is probably raining throughout the province as we receive an average of only 25 cm of precipitation annually.

Your lakeside accommodations on Kamloops Lake are charming and comfortable. Individually decorated, all nine rooms and suites have a private entrance, private ensuite bathroom, color cable TV, telephone with data port, clock radio, coffee maker, complimentary coffee and tea, and luxurious amenities. While all  rooms are non-smoking, smoking is allowed outdoors on your balcony or patio.  Lakeside Country Inn is a triple diamond CAA/AAA approved accommodation.
