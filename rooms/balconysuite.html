One Bedroom Kitchen Suite with Balcony

Our one bedroom kitchen suite has a queen bed in a separate bedroom and a queen hide-a-bed in the living area. The charming country decor creates a home away from home. The room features a larger private balcony overlooking the gardens down to the lake. It is complete with a bistro table and chairs. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, stove with an oven, microwave, dishes, cutlery, basic pots, toaster, kettle, and coffeemaker. Coffee and tea are complimentary. Enjoy a memorable evening on your balcony.

Room rate is $129.50 to $189.50, depending on the date you will be visiting. Bed and Breakfast is an additional $9.95 per person.
