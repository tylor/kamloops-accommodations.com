Lakeview Kitchen Suite on the Top Floor with Balcony

Bright and spacious, this room on the top floor has two queen beds and a kitchen off to the side. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, stovetop and microwave, dishes, cutlery, basic pots, toaster; kettle, coffeemaker and complimentary coffee and tea. The view of Kamloops Lake from your larger balcony, complete with table and chairs, is truly wonderful.

Room rate is $129.50 to $189.50, depending on the date you will be visiting. Bed and Breakfast is an additional $9.95 per person.
