Veranda Room with Queen Bed

This quaint and cozy room has a queen bed and white wicker furniture with a charm of its own. Adirondack chairs on the veranda outside your room are a comfortable spot for a cup of tea. While it does not have a lake view, you can still enjoy the lake, as there are many loungers and Adirondack chairs at the beach and in the gardens overlooking the lake.

Room rate is $79.50 to $119.50, depending on the date you will be visiting. Bed and Breakfast is an additional $9.95 per person.
