Rooms & Rates

Lakeside Country Inn's accommodations are charming and comfortable.

Individually decorated, all nine rooms and suites have a private entrance, private ensuite bathroom, colour cable TV, telephone with data port, clock radio, coffee maker, complimentary coffee and tea, and luxurious amenities.

While all rooms are non-smoking, smoking is allowed outdoors on your balcony or patio.

Lakeside Country Inn is a triple diamond CAA/AAA approved accommodation and was recommended in Country magazine and Hidden British Columbia.

"This is a perfect stop on the way to or from a week in the interior... it's well off the highway and most rooms face toward the lake, so serenity reigns." - Eric Lucas, Hidden British Columbia

Room rates are quoted in Canadian dollars. Taxes are not included in the room rate.

Choose from a lake view room or a larger kitchen suite. Bed and breakfast rates and room only rates are available. A full continental breakfast, including a wonderful selection of fruit salad, yogurt, cereals, muffins, and our English cream scones is served in the glass enclosed Garden Cafe during the summer. It is a relaxing way to start the day,  meet your hosts and other guests.
