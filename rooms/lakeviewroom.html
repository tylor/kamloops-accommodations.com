Lakeview Room with Balcony

Our charming lake view rooms with a queen bed or two twin beds feature a balcony overlooking Kamloops Lake and the gardens. Enjoy the peaceful evening, sunset's glow and your morning cup of coffee from your private balcony. All rooms have a private bath and entrance, coffeemaker, complimentary coffee and tea, and bar fridge. Choose from a room on the top floor or the main level. A continental breakfast is served in our glass enclosed Garden Cafe from May to October and is a relaxing way to start your day.

Main level rooms are $89.50 to $139.50, top floor rooms are $99.50 - $149.50 for room only; depending on the date you will be visiting. Bed and breakfast is $9.95 per person.
