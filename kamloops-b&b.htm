Come to the Lakeside: The Kamloops b&b of choice

Lakeside Country b&b is located on beautiful Kamloops Lake near the small town of Savona, west of the City of Kamloops. The delightful Kamloops B&B is easy to find; just a short trip off the #1 Trans Canada Highway.

Visitors to the Bamp;b love our hot, sunny summers; the weather is wonderful. Average daily temperatures in June-August are 25-29 degrees C, cooling to 22 degrees through September.

Features of the Kamloops B&B
- Lake view b&b rooms with private balconies overlooking the lake and gardens
- Our Kamloops B&b has spacious shaded lawns & fragrant flower gardens where you can relax with a book in a lounger.
- Swim & suntan on our safe & private beach
- Picnic and roast marshmallows at the beach
- Enjoy your morning cup of coffee or a picnic lunch at the patio tables in the gardens.
- Enjoy our abundance of native birds including wild yellow canaries, baltimore orioles and hummingbirds. Dozens of trumpeter and whistling swans winter on the lake.
- Kitchen b&b suites are available
- All b&b Rooms have on-suite bathrooms and private entrances
- Colour cable T.V./Telephones with data port
- Discover spectacular Kamloops Lake. Canoe, paddle boat, and fishing boat and motor rentals
- Fish for Kamloops & Rainbow trout
- Garden Café open during summer for a continental breakfast
