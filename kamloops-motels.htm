Kamloops Lakeside - motel style lodging in BC Canada

Lakeside Country Inn offers motel style accommodation which is charming and comfortable. All nine rooms and suites have a private entrance, private ensuite bathroom, colour cable TV, telephone with data port, clock radio, coffee maker access to all amenities. Lakeside Country Inn is a triple diamond CAA/AAA approved accommodation.

Lake view rooms have either a queen bed or two twins. Their balconies overlook Kamloops Lake and the gardens.

The quaint and cozy Veranda Room room has a queen bed and white wicker furniture. Adirondack chairs on the veranda outside your room are a comfortable spot for a cup of tea. It has no lake view, but you can still enjoy the waterfront from the many loungers and Adirondack chairs at the beach and in the gardens.

The Small Kitchen Suite with Patio is a cozy kitchenette which opens onto a small patio on the garden level. A patio table and chairs make it a wonderful spot to enjoy the evening's conversation, or relax with a book in the loungers on the lawn just outside your room. This country decorated suite has a double bed and a small hide-a-bed. The suite sleeps up to three adults or two adults and two small children. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, stove and oven, basic pots, dishes, utensils, coffeemaker, complimentary coffee and tea.

Our one bedroom kitchen suite has a queen bed in a separate bedroom and a queen hide-a-bed in the living area. The charming country decor creates a home away from home. Complete with a bistro table and chairs, a larger private balcony overlooks the gardens out to the lake. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge; stovetop and oven; microwave; dishes; cutlery; basic pots; toaster; kettle; and coffeemaker. Bring your own groceries or order a pizza and enjoy a memorable dinner on your balcony.

The Bright and spacious Lakeview Kitchen Suite, this room on the top floor has two queen beds and a kitchen off to the side. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge; stovetop and microwave; blue willow dishes, cutlery, basic pots; toaster; kettle; coffeemaker and complimentary coffee and tea. The view of Kamloops Lake from your balcony is truly wonderful.

Our two bedroom kitchen suite is a favourite with families. The cozy country decorated bedrooms have a double bed in one bedroom and two twin beds in the other. The motel style kitchen/living area has a loveseat, dinette, armoire with cable TV and telephone. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, stove, basic pots , dishes, utensils, toaster, coffeemaker, complimentary coffee and tea. The suite opens onto a small patio on the back lawn with a patio table and patio chairs where you may eat your dinner outside in the fresh air. Bring your swimming suit, some groceries, and a good book--and relax!
