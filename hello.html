Rooms and Rates

Relax and Enjoy Our Charming Country Decor and Hospitality

Lakeside Country Inn's accommodations are charming and comfortable. Individually decorated, all nine rooms and suites have a private entrance, private ensuite bathroom, colour cable TV, telephone with data port, clock radio, coffee maker, complimentary coffee and tea, and luxurious amenities. While all rooms are non-smoking, smoking is allowed outdoors on your balcony or patio.

Lakeside Country Inn is a triple diamond CAA/AAA approved accommodation and was recommended in Hidden British Columbia and "Country Magazine."  

Room rates are quoted in Canadian dollars. Taxes are not included in the room rate. Visitors to Canada are eligible for a refund of the GST.

Choose from a lakeview room with a private balcony or a larger kitchen suite with a patio or balcony. Both Bed and Breakfast rates or Room Only rates are available. A full continental breakfast, including a wonderful selection of fruit salad, yogurt, cereals, muffins, scones and bagels with cream cheese, is served in the glass enclosed Garden Cafe during the summer. It is a relaxing way to start the day and meet other guests from all over the world.

Lakeview Room with Balcony
Our charming lake view rooms with a queen bed or two twin beds have a balcony overlooking Kamloops Lake and the gardens. Enjoy the peaceful evening, sunset's glow and your morning cup of coffee from your private balcony. All rooms have a private bath, coffeemaker, complimentary coffee and tea and bar fridge. A continental breakfast is served in our glass enclosed Garden Cafe from May to October and is a relaxing way to start your day. Rooms are available on the main level or top floor.

Room rate for top floor room is $89.50 to $129.50,  Room rate for the main level is $79.50 to $119.50, depending on the season.  Bed and Breakfast is an additional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.

Veranda Room with Queen Bed
This quaint and cozy room has a queen bed and white wicker furniture with a charm of its own. Adirondack chairs on the veranda outside your room are a comfortable spot for a cup of tea. While it does not have a lake view, you can still enjoy the lake, as there are loungers and Adirondack chairs at the beach and in the gardens overlooking the lake.

Room rate is $69.50 to $99.50, depending on the date you will visit.  Bed and breakfast is an additional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.

Small Kitchen Suite with Patio
Our quaint and cozy kitchenette opens onto a small patio on the garden level. A patio table and chairs make it a wonderful spot to enjoy the evening's conversation. Or relax with a book in the loungers on the lawn just outside your room. This country decorated suite has a double bed and a small hide-a-bed. The suite sleeps up to three adults or two adults and two small children. The equipped kitchen  has a fridge, stove and oven, coffeemaker, toaster and kettle, as well as complimentary coffee and tea. Cable TV and telephone are standard in all our rooms.

Room rate is $89.50 to $134.50, depending on the date you will visit. Bed and Breakfast is an additional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.

One Bedroom Kitchen Suite with Balcony
Our one bedroom kitchen suite has a queen bed in a separate bedroom and a queen hide-a-bed in the living area. The charming country decor creates a home away from home. Complete with a bistro table and chairs, a larger private balcony overlooks the gardens out to the lake. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge; stovetop and oven; microwave; dishes; cutlery; basic pots; toaster; kettle; and coffeemaker. Coffee and tea are complimentary.

Room rate is $119.50 to $169.50, depending on the date. Bed and Breakfast is an additional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.

Lakeview Kitchen Suite on the Top Floor with Balcony
Bright and spacious, this room on the top floor has two queen beds and a kitchen off to the side. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge; stovetop and microwave; blue willow dishes, cutlery, basic pots; toaster; kettle; coffeemaker and complimentary coffee and tea. The view of Kamloops Lake from your balcony is truly wonderful.

Room rate is $119.50 to $174.50, depending on the date you will visit. Bed and breakfast is an additional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.

Two Bedroom Kitchen Suite with Patio
Our two bedroom kitchen suite is a favourite with families. The cozy country decorated bedrooms have a double bed in one bedroom and two twin beds in the other. The kitchen/living area has a loveseat, dinette, armoire with cable TV and telephone. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, stove, basic pots , dishes, utensils, toaster, coffeemaker, complimentary coffee and tea. The suite opens onto a small patio on the back lawn. A patio table and patio chairs invite you to eat your dinner outside in the fresh air. Bring your swimming suit, some groceries, and a good book--and relax!

Room rate is $119.50 to $174.50, depending on the date you will visit. Bed and breakfast is an addtional $8.95 per person. See chart below for rates.
